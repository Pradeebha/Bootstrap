ADMISSION

CHRIST (Deemed to be University), Bengaluru is secular in its outlook and welcomes students from all castes, religions, creed and languages to be a part of the Christite family.

ACADEMICS

The Academic engagement at CHRIST (Deemed to be University) is intense and rigorous. The deaneries ,centers and offices support the departments in their academic innovation.

CAMPUS

CHRIST (Deemed to be University) has three campuses in Bengaluru. The Main campus on the Hosur Road, one in Kengeri and the latest campus on the Bannerghatta road.
